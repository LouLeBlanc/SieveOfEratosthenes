Welcome to the Sieve Of Eratosthenes Docker application.
This application will provide you a list of prime numbers up to a specified
upper bound, and will provide a summary afterward.
Simple execute the command:
  SieveOfEratosthenes
and provide the upper bound for the sieve to work with.
